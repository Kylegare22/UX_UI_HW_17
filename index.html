About Me



I'm Kyle, a UX Designer and Tech expert based out of Faribault, MN.

My career path taken me from Sales Analytics and Operations to a role leading teams in Ecommerce by way of UX Design. This current creative chapter allows me to be the intermediary between the user and the bottom line, where I can help affect change on both fronts.

I love technology, cities, and gaming, but I spend a lot of time daydreaming about nature and quiet spaces.
