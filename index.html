About Me



I'm Kyle, a UX Designer and Tech expert based out of Faribault, MN.

With a background in UX design, writing, and photography, I'm on a mission to craft engaging content while breaking down tech barriers. Graduated from Minnesota State University, Mankato with a Mass Communications major and a Film Studies minor, I now thrive as a Communications Liaison at Lakeville Area Schools.

Currently, as an Audio/Visual Tech Support Specialist, I'm expanding my expertise in communications and technology. But my true passion lies in creating projects, showcasing talents, and inspiring others.

This website is my creative playground, where I experiment with new ideas and connect with fellow creators. Join me in making technology work for everyone.
